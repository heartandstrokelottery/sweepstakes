Consumer protection and privacy

The Heart and Stroke Lottery never asks winners to pay to claim their prize. If you're told to pay, contact our customer service at (647) 391-4881. To avoid lottery fraud, keep a list of the lotteries you've entered and ask anyone who says you've won to tell you exactly when and where you entered. Remember, in Canada, you don’t have to pay fees or taxes to get your prize, so don’t trust anyone who asks for money. Make sure emails come from ontarioheartandstrokes.ca@gmail.com, not from places like Yahoo or Gmail. If you get mail, check for the official letterhead and make sure the address matches the one on our website. Also, look out for bad spelling or grammar, as real messages from us are always well-written. For more tips on staying safe from lottery scams and identity theft, visit our Consumer Protection page.

The Heart and Stroke Foundation is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect personal information such as your name, address, and email to process donations, provide updates, and improve our services. Your information is kept confidential and is not shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. You can access or update your information by contacting us at [privacy email or phone number]. For more details, please read our full Privacy Policy.

Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) applies to all organizations, including registered charities, when they send commercial electronic messages (CEMs). However, certain communications from charities, such as those related to fundraising, may be exempt from some CASL requirements.

At the Heart and Stroke Foundation, we strive to comply with CASL by obtaining consent for our communications and providing clear unsubscribe options. For more information on CASL and its application to charities, please visit the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) website.

To unsubscribe from our mailing list, you can:

  • Click the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email you receive from us.
  • Contact our customer service department at (647) 391-4881 to request removal from the mailing list.
  • Visit our subscription management page to update your preferences.

Please note that it may take up to 10 business days for your unsubscribe request to be processed. You may still receive some communications during this period.

How to play & license numbers

The Heart & Stroke Classic Lottery is a major fundraising event offering participants the chance to win substantial prizes while supporting heart disease and stroke research. It features multiple grand prizes, including two $1 million cash prizes, as well as luxury vehicles, boats, and other valuable items. With thousands of prizes available, the odds of winning are approximately 1 in 2. Tickets are available in various packs, such as $100 each, 3 for $250, or 5 for $375.

The 50/50 Lottery is an additional opportunity to win big while supporting the Heart & Stroke Foundation. In this lottery, the winner receives 50% of the total jackpot accumulated from ticket sales, with a minimum guaranteed payout of $500,000. The more tickets sold, the larger the jackpot grows. Tickets are sold separately from the Classic Lottery and are available in packs, such as 5 for $25, 15 for $50, or 25 for $75.

The Heart & Stroke July Money Lottery is a special event held throughout the month of July, offering daily draws with cash prizes. Each day in July, there is one $10,000 cash prize and one hundred $50 gift cards awarded, providing over 100 chances to win daily. Tickets for this lottery are sold separately and come in packs like 4 for $20, 12 for $50, 20 for $75, or 35 for $125.
