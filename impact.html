Researcher highlights

Protecting hearts and pregnancies through research.

New heart disease in pregnancy is becoming more common. Dr. Rohan D'Souza is leading a collaborative research network to monitor and prevent cardiovascular events during and after pregnancy!

Read the story

Developing breakthrough therapies to repair damaged hearts.

Dr. Emilio Alarcón's team has developed an innovative peptide-based material that mimics natural heart proteins to heal and restore heart function after a heart attack. Engineered in a lab but inspired by nature, this groundbreaking approach goes beyond mechanical support, helping the heart regenerate naturally, thanks to support from Heart & Stroke funding.
